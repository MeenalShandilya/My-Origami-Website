Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons can recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph. So do they just pretend to be dumb?
  2. They are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' â€“ a test of self recognition. Hey, pigeon- you been hiding your intelligence from us, eh?
  3. Pigeons can fly at average speeds of up to 77.6 mph but have been recorded flying at 92.5 mph. Smart AND fast.
  4. Pigeons have fought alongside humans in World War I and II. As messengers, of course. Not soldiers.
  5. Pigeons have "feelings". You can see that when intruders are in their nest's proximity. Male pigeons will actually begin screeching and attempt to distract the invader through multiple flying motion. Cool.
  6. Pigeon's vision is far superior to that of humans and most animals on the planet. These creatures do not only have a wider visual field; they are also able to perceive ultraviolet lights and colours that are invisible to humans. I want those eyes. Right now.
