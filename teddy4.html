Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902. Thank God they didn't name them Roosevelt bears.
  2. Jackie Miley, from Hilly City, South Dakota is a teddy bear collector. She has the world’s biggest teddy bear collection, totaling 8,026 bear friends in 2012.
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  4. 9th September is celebrated as National Teddy Bear Day (I guess not in India, though).
  5. Hugging teddy bears makes us feel calm and soothed because of the release of the hormone oxytocin. I'll go and hug mine!
