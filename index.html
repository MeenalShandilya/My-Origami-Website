Interesting Facts About Camels

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes. Don't their eyes look beautiful?
  2. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms. Well, I wouldn't want to breathe in sand either.
  3. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse. Ever heard of camel-ride racing? Me neither.
  4. When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go. Pretty thirsty, it is.
  5. Don’t make a camel angry – they can spit as a way to distract whatever they think is a threat. I wouldn't want camel saliva on my face.
  6. Camels are social animals; their groups include as many as 30 individuals. They would become good friends, I guess.
