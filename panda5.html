Interesting Facts About Pandas

  1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown). Which is your favourite?
  2. Pandas have so many fans because they look cute. Certainly, they're chubby.
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day. I hope I were a panda.
  4. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day! Ever wondered why they're so fat?
  5. Pandas have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food. Woah.
